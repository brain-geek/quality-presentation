External quality

External quality This is the quality of the product that is perceived by the users of the product. This includes the stability of the product, the ease of use of it, the interaction design and the way it conforms to the requirements

Internal quality

This is the quality of the source code, the quality of the architecture, the quality of the development process around the product, etc. Anything that is not perceivable by the users of the software.
